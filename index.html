For assisting policy development and program management of DSWD – RSCC XI – this is a data collection system which primarily aims to eliminate a manual or paper-based operations mainly for the monitoring of the children’s adoption and foster care related data. This also adds up a more secured database. The adoption data, includes race/ethnicity of those adopted as well as lengths of time to important milestones in the adoption process. SMSegurado: Mobile Application

SMSegurado is a mobile application designed to detect smishing attacks in SMS content using a BERT model. The app employs two models: the first, mBERT, automatically scans incoming messages to identify potential smishing threats, while the second, DistilBERT, performs a more detailed re-scan of the text content. This innovative project has gained recognition and will be showcased at ESTCON 2024, taking place in Malaysia from September 11–13, 2024, under the guidance of our advisor.

Human Resource Management System

The Human Resource Management System (HRMS) is a web-based platform designed to optimize HR processes, supporting three user roles: Super Admin, Admin, and Employee. Built with Dom Snappy composer, PUSHER for real-time notifications, and a time scheduler, the system allows Admins to manage employee records by adding, updating, and removing personal and employment details. They can also track attendance through clock-in and clock-out timesheets, update leave records such as credits and balances, notify employees about celebrations, contract renewals, events, and meetings, and generate reports on employee data, leave summaries, and attendance. The Super Admin module features a data analytics dashboard that displays employee demographics, turnover and retention rates, and growth rates. Employee users can update personal information, view attendance records, clock-in and clock-out, check leave balances, and receive notifications. The system will be tested for functionality, reliability, and usability to ensure optimal performance.
